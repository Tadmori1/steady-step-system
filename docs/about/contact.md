## What to Send

### Experiment Ideas
"I want to see you test [specific behavior] for [timeframe]."

Example: "Test going caffeine-free for 7 days and track energy levels."

### Real Problems (3-Day Experiments)
"I struggle with [specific issue]. What's a small experiment I can try?"

Example: "I can't focus for more than 5 minutes. What should I do?"

### Challenge Me
"Can you [specific challenge] and document it?"

Example: "Can you learn a new striking combo in 3 days?"

### General Questions
Ask anything about the system, training, habits, discipline, or content production.

## Response Time

- **Form submissions:** We review these weekly (Sundays) and respond to selected questions via content or direct reply
- **Questions featured in videos:** If we turn your question into a video, we'll notify you (if you provided contact info)
- **Experiment voting:** Community votes on next week's experiments via YouTube Community posts (Sundays)

## Community Guidelines

Before submitting, please review our [Community Guidelines](community-guidelines.md) to understand what we can and cannot address.

**We do NOT provide:**
- Medical diagnosis or treatment
- Personal training plans
- One-on-one coaching (currently)
- Nutrition prescriptions

**We DO provide:**
- Educational experiments you can test yourself
- Small, measurable behavior changes
- Systems for discipline and consistency
- Skill-building progressions (especially Skills Track)
