  - Production Playbook: Filming Setup, Editing Rules, Sound & Voice, Visual Language, QA Checklist, Posting & Iteration
  - Research Shelf: Biomechanics Fundamentals, Force/Impulse/Momentum, Linear vs Angular, Work/Energy/Power
  - About: About Steady Step, About Me, Lineage & Styles, Safety & Scope, Contact

## Version Numbering

We use semantic versioning: `MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH`

- **MAJOR:** Breaking changes to site structure or navigation
- **MINOR:** New pages, sections, or significant content additions
- **PATCH:** Content updates, bug fixes, minor improvements
